How long does it take?
Most clients see their first AI systems up and running within 2-4 weeks. Larger projects take longer, but we focus on quick wins first so you start seeing value right away.
What kinds of businesses do you work with?
We work with small and mid-size businesses across industries—consulting firms, professional services, sales teams, operations-heavy businesses. If your team spends time on repetitive tasks, we can probably help.
What's the difference between consulting and the cohort?
The cohort teaches you a framework so you can use AI yourself. Consulting means we do it for you—we build the systems, train your team, and stay on as your AI partner. Some clients do both.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes. AI changes fast, and we don't just build something and disappear. We stay available for questions, updates, and new opportunities as they come up.
What if I'm not sure consulting is what I need?
That's exactly what the strategy call is for. We'll talk through where you are, what you're trying to do, and honestly recommend the best path—whether that's consulting, the cohort, or the self-paced course.
